Kanao Tsuyuri

About Kanao Tsuyuri

Kanao Tsuyuri is one of the most important side characters in the Demon Slayers series. She is a Demon Slayer in the Demon Slayers corps. She is also Shinobu Kocho's adopted sister. In the end (refer from manga), she is married to Tanjiro Kamado.

Kanao's Friends

Kanao-of course, as a character-interacts with other characters throughout the series. To know more about the characters Kanao interacts to, click on the link below.

  1. Tanjiro Kamado, is a professional teammate in the Demon Slayer Corps, and later on a husband.
  2. Kanae and Shinobu Kocho, are sisters who adopted her.
  3. Aoi, is a helper at Butterfly Mansion and one of the closest person to Kanao.

Kanao's Characteristics and Abilities

  • Kanao has a low-self esteem. As the result, she rarely talks and finds herself hard to make a decision.
  • Kanao has immense stamina and endurance. She has been physically trained before joining Demon Slayer Corps.
  • Kanao has an ultimate skill: "Concentration: Constant (全ぜん集しゅう中ちゅう・常じょう中ちゅう Zen Shūchū Jōchū)."
